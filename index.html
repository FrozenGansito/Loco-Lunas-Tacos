Our Story

Family Recipes.
Street-Level Love.

Loco Lunas Tacos started in 2018 when cousins Marco and Diego decided their family's Oaxacan recipes were too good to stay in the kitchen. What began as a weekend pop-up became a city staple, known for long lines, loud music, and tacos that keep people coming back.

Every tortilla is pressed by hand. Every marinade is made from scratch. We source our produce from local farms and our chiles from the same importers our grandmother used.
